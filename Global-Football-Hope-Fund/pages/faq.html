Frequently Asked Questions

Is participation free?

Yes. Registration and predictions are completely free.

Do I have to donate?

No. Donations are voluntary and not required to participate or win.

How are winners selected?

Winners are selected based on prediction accuracy and leaderboard points in a skill-based contest.

Is this affiliated with FIFA?

No. This is an independent community project with no official affiliation.

Where do donations go?

70% goes to children's charities, 20% to prizes, and 10% to operations, with all donations publicly visible.

Is everything transparent?

Yes. Wallets, donations, and reports will be publicly displayed.
