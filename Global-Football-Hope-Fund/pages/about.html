About Us

Global Football Hope Fund is a worldwide football community initiative created to bring fans together through free prediction competitions and voluntary cryptocurrency donations that support children's charities.

Our mission is simple: turn football passion into real-world impact. We believe every fan, no matter where they are, should have the opportunity to contribute to something meaningful while enjoying the game.

Our Mission

Free Participation

Anyone can register and join the competition without paying to enter.

Skill-Based Predictions

Participants compete through football knowledge rather than paid raffle-style entry.

Transparent Giving

Optional donations support children’s charities, and the fund allocation is published clearly.

Why This Model Matters

This project is built around trust, transparency, and social impact. By keeping entry free, making donations optional, and publishing the charity flow clearly, we aim to create a campaign that feels legitimate, global, and sustainable from the start.
